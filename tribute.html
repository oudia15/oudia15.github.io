Tribute to Dr Adama T Diarra

Result of hard work and determination

LIFE TIMELINE

Education

Born in April 26 1956 of Tiemoko Diarra and Yaye Traore, Adama T Diarra realized early on that education is the key to success. He was the only one out of six(6) kids to make it to college. He obtained a scholarship and studied in Ukraine where he obtained a masters and then a PhD in hydrology.

Professional Career

Dr Diarra started his career as an independant consultant and later became a professor at ENI(Ecole National d'Ingenieurs), the only engineering school in Mali where he later became the head of the department of hydrology. In 2008 he was nominated by the minister of water as a technical advisor. He held that position until he became the first director General Chairman of SOMAPEP.

Political Career

Dr Diarra on top of being an accomplished scholar and Administrator is also a politician. He is member is ADEMA PASJ(3rd most powerful polical party in Mali) where he holds the position of political secretary. Due to his political involvement, Dr Diarra became in 2007 the 1st vice President of CENI(Comission national d'Independant)

You can see some of the work Dr Diarra has accomplished with his team since he became head of SOMAPEP at Somapep.org
